ABOUT US

Our country is growing day in day out through the construction of more roads and buildings.
Although these kind of growth is happening, it is not felt countrywide but rather felt in some places more than others.
Therfore, using this web application, I-reporter, one is able to bring growth to their home areas by pointing out the facilities they lack in the area.
This web app could also assist the government offices in pointing out areas which require more growth in infrastructure than others.
